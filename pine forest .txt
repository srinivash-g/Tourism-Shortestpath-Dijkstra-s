The soaring pines, breathtaking views and a pleasant atmosphere makes it a perfect base 
 for filming various movies It is a famous shooting spot in Ooty.
 Several Bollywood hit films such as Qayamat se Qayamat tak, Khuda Gawah,
 Sadma, Dil and Deewana have been shot here. This picturesque location has so 
 much to offer for tourists of all ages and one of the magical wonders is its scenic 
 canvas of tall and magnificent trees.
