Green Nest resort offers accommodation in Kotagiri. Guests can enjoy the 
 on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.Each room 
 comes with a flat screen TV with cable channels. Certain units have a seating area
 where you can relax. Certain rooms include views of the mountains or garden. 
 Every room is fitted with a private bathroom fitted with a shower.
 There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Guests can head to the terrace to enjoy 
 .the surroundings. 
Free use of bicycles is available at this resort and the area is popular for cycling.
It is 6 km from Longwood Shola, 15 km from Kodanadu View Point and 20 km from Cathrine 
 Waterfalls.The Kotagiri Bus Station is 7 km away, Coonoor Railway Station is 15 km and 
 the Coimbatore International Airport is 65 km away.
 The in-house restaurant serves Indian, Chinese and continental delights.
 Room service is available for in-room dining comforts.
