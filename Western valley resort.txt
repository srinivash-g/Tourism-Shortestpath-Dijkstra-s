Situated in Ooty, 14.2 km from Ooty Lake, Western Valley Resorts features a 
 restaurant. The resort has a barbecue and views of the mountains, and guests 
 can enjoy a meal at the restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.

 relax in after a busy day. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the lake 
 or garden. Each room includes a private bathroom with a bath and bidet, with 
 slippers provided. There is dry cleaning services at the property.
