 Thunder world also called as Dinosaur park in ooty. Thunder world ooty known 
 for its Jurassic Jungle, Haunted house, Vortex ride and many more attractions. 
 Thunder World is loved by kids of all ages and is one of the most fun 
 family places in Ooty. The use of sound and technology to make the thunder 
 world theme park seem life-like is what makes the place one-of-a-kind. 
 The various themes of horror, history and immersive technology, adds to the fun-factor.
