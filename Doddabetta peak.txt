Doddabetta Peak is Nestled amidst the Blue Mountains the quintessential
 town of Ooty allures visitors in abundance to witness its postcard picture perfect beauty. 
  Nilgiris are more popular as Blue Mountains as it is believed to be cloaked with the blue 
   haze of the rare Kurinji blue flowers that bloom once in 12 years.
   View from Doddabetta Peak The marvellous views of the sweeping forests of Bandipur National 
    Park and the open highlands of Mysore are best enjoyed from the summit.
