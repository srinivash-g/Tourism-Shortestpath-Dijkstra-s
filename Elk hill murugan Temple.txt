Perched atop the Elk Hill in Ooty, amidst picturesque backdrop and a resplendent 
setting, Elk Murugan Temple is a revered Hindu temple enshrined by Lord Muruga.
 Besides the gorgeous landscape and astounding panoramic vistas,the hill also boasts 
 of rich and flourishing wildlife and bounteous nature. The highlight of the place is
 a 40 feet tall statue of Lord Muruga constructed in brick and mortar that stands tall 
 outside the enclosures of the temple and attracts pilgrims and devotees from far and wide.
